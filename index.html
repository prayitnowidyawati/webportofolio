Hello, I'm Widya

Educator, Writer and Traveler

let's expLore!


Teaching with grace, traveling with wonder, writing with soul.

For more than ten years, I have dedicated my journey to education—helping students from diverse backgrounds build confidence, prepare for exams, and step into global opportunities. With a degree in English Education , I specialized in English teaching, Indonesian Teaching, academic and personal development coaching. My work extends beyond the classroom. I am the author of several Indie English books designed to make language learning accessible and empowering. The Metamorphosis, a self development book based on personal stories.

From the freezing cold of snow in Yerevan and the scorching heat of dessert sun in Doha, cultures continue to shape me, weaving new perspectives into writing that become my expressions. Apart from that, I am also a fan of Art; crafting ,painting, and drawing, very much so with savoring my palate with a good food and inventing new cooking recipes!

I believe education is more than academics—it is a journey of becoming, a path of self-discovery, and a bridge to a wider world.

Expertise

My key areas of expertise.

The Intersection of communication, confidence, and abundance. I support people in finding their voice, expressing themselves with clarity, and trusting their abilities. My work recognizes that communication rooted in self awareness will naturally grow confidence that opens access to academic success, meaningful professional opportunities, and a more abundant engagement with learning and life.

Language Teaching (English & Indonesian)

I teach English from early learners to advanced levels, focusing on clear, confident communication. I also work in BIPA (Indonesian for Foreign Learners), helping international students develop accurate language skills and cultural understanding.

⁠Project Management & Curriculum Design

I design and manage structured learning programmes with clear goals. My curriculum work creates simple, coherent learning pathways that support progress and engagement.

Educational Consultancy

I support students and families in making informed academic choices. My consultancy helps align strengths, interests, and long-term study goals.

⁠Personal Development

Personal development is central to my work. I help learners build self-awareness, confidence, and communication skills that support both learning and personal growth.

Writing

Writing is a core part of my practice. I create clear and accessible educational content that communicates ideas with purpose.
